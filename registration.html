Author Guidelines

ICETTSF 2025 invites full-length original research contributions from engineering professionals from industries, R&D organizations, academic institutions, government departments, and research scholars from across the world.

  • The conference proceedings will be published in Scopus indexed Springer book series.
  • Full-length original research contributions and review articles as per the given paper format shall be submitted.
  • The manuscript should not have been published in any journals/magazines or conference proceedings and must not be under review in any of them.
  • The manuscript should contain the name of the corresponding author with an email ID and the affiliation of all authors.
  • All submitted manuscripts will be sent for peer review, and the corresponding author will be notified of the outcome of the review process.
  • ICETTS 2025 will not publish any paper that has not been registered.
  • If a complaint regarding the authorship of a paper is received and found to be valid, the paper will be immediately withdrawn from publication.
  • The editorial board holds the right to reject any paper for publication in an international journal after the second round of review (post-conference). If a paper is rejected, the conference registration fee will not be refunded.
  • Submit the manuscripts through the "Register Now" button.
  • Submitted research work must be original in content and free from plagiarism.
  • Refer to the brochure for more information on formatting the abstract and paper.
